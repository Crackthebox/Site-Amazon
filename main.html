    The Amazon rainforest is a global biodiversity hotspot, home to countless plant, animal, and insect species found nowhere else. Its dense forests and vast river systems sustain jaguars, macaws, pink river dolphins, and millions of insects. This rich ecosystem supports Indigenous communities and plays a crucial role in Earth’s climate balance.
